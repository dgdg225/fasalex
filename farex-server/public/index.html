⬆ PLACE COIN BESIDE PRODUCE ⬆
1
Use any ₹2 coin
Standard Indian ₹2 coin = 25mm diameter. This is your permanent size reference. Keep it in your pocket during scanning.
2
Place beside produce
Put the coin flat on the same surface as the produce, touching or 1-2cm away. Both coin and produce must be in the same frame.
3
Camera distance: 15-25cm
Hold your phone 15-25cm above the produce. Good lighting is important — use LED flash in low light. Avoid shadows.
4
Hold steady, one tap
Tap once to capture. DeepGazer™ AI detects the coin automatically and calculates exact size. No manual marking needed.
✅ One-time per phone — KrushiEx learns your phone's camera characteristics and applies a correction constant for accurate measurements every scan.

📱 LiDAR phones (iPhone Pro) get ±1mm accuracy. Other phones get ±3-5mm with coin — suitable for all fruit and vegetable grading.
